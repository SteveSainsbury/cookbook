Oat Pancakes

Ingredient Quantity
Milk 3/4 cup
Rolled Oats 3/4 cup
Sugar 3 tbsp
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Egg 1
Flour 1/2 Cup
Baking Powder 2 tsp
Ground Cinnamon 1 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp

Pour milk over oats and allow to stand for 10 minutes Add sugar, oil and egg and beat with a fork. Sift in remaining dry ingredients and stir to form a smooth batter If necessary, add 1-2 tablespoons of milk to thin the mixture Fry about 1/8 of the mixture for around 2 minutes each side until done
