Basque-Style Baked Cheese-Cake

  • 500g Fresh Ricotta
  • 120ml Cream
  • 6 eggs, beaten
  • 250g caster sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest

Mix the ricotta and cream until homogenous. Add the eggs and continue mixing until homogenous. Mix in the sugar and lemon zest. Butter a 11" pie dish and place into an oven pre-heated to around 180Â° for 40 minutes.
